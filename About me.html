About me

Biography | Passion | Experience

My name is Dena Churchman and I am 28 years old. I am currently in my third year of my B.ED - ECD: Foundation Phase through Unisa. This website contains my online portfolio and all I have learnt about becoming a professional teacher.

Children are my biggest passion and I have a natural ability to relate to them. I love being in an environment that allows for creativity and experimentation from a young age. I especially love working with children who have learning barriers or special needs.

I have worked extensively with children at different periods in my life, some form underprivileged situations, some special needs and in mainstream school environments too. I have Kearny much about interacting, teaching, caring for, and facilitating children.
